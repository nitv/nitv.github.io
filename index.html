Nitin Verma

I am a doctoral student at the School of Information at The University of Texas at Austin. My broad research interest is investigating the notion of trust in information and information technology. My dissertation research focuses on how AI-based synthetic audiovisual media (such as deepfake videos) might influence trust in visual media.
